Ammonium Sulphate (Mono)

Grade: 98%

Ammonium Sulphate (Mono) is a premium, high-purity inorganic fertilizer that provides an excellent source of readily available nitrogen and sulfur. It is essential for lowering soil pH in alkaline soils and promoting rapid vegetative growth, making it ideal for a wide variety of crops including tea, rice, and vegetables.

Key Benefits

  • Highly soluble for quick plant uptake
  • Lowers pH in alkaline and calcareous soils
  • Essential sulfur promotes enzyme and vitamin synthesis
  • Free-flowing crystalline structure for easy application

Usage Instructions

Suitable for basal dressing and top dressing. Application rates vary based on crop and soil test results. Consult your agronomist for specific dosage.
