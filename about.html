About Hetshavi Fertilizer

Discover our roots, our mission, and our passion for creating a sustainable agricultural future.

Our Story

Nurturing Nature, Empowering Farmers

Founded in 2011, Hetshavi Fertilizer started with a simple vision: to bridge the gap between traditional farming wisdom and modern agritech. Over the years, we have grown from a small consultancy to a comprehensive provider of organic agricultural inputs.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, eco-friendly agricultural solutions that enhance yield while preserving the earth's natural resources.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in sustainable agriculture, ensuring food security for future generations.

Our Journey

2011 - The Beginning

Started as a small local farm advisory service in a single county.

2015 - Product Launch

Introduced our first line of 100% organic fertilizers.

2020 - National Expansion

Expanded operations to serve farmers nationwide with modern tech solutions.

Today - Leading the Change

Pioneering sustainable agritech for over 50,000 farmers globally.
